Frequently Asked Questions

Henna is a natural paste made which is applied to the skin and left to dry. Once it is removed,it darkens to a medium to deep brown and fades in 5-12 days.

Henna is made from natural Rajasthani Henna Powder, Sugar, Essential Oil (Eucalyptus, Lavendar, Cajeput etc.) and Water.
